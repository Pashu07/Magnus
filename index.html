About Magnes

Founded in 2017 by three passionate engineers, Magnes emerged with a mission to build the most powerful electric go kart in India. Fuelled by their dedication, our team created the world's first smart and integrated electric go kart, setting a new standard in the industry. Magnes is a prominent player in the go karting market, located in Mumbai, India. Our expertise lies in designing, developing, and delivering cutting-edge go karts, as well as providing comprehensive go kart track design and consultancy services to clients worldwide. We are driven by our commitment to excellence and innovation, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner in the industry. Throughout our journey, Magnes has successfully executed numerous projects, leaving a trail of exhilarated customers and unforgettable memories. Our portfolio showcases a diverse range of indoor and outdoor go karting tracks, each crafted with precision and meticulous attention to detail. From standalone tracks to those integrated within family entertainment centers, our team of skilled engineers, designers, and technicians work tirelessly to ensure an unparalleled go karting experience
